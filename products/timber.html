Miro Forestry’s sawn timber boards are available in both furniture and construction grades, in lengths up to 6.5 meters.

Our modern thin-kerf sawmill technology allows us to produce a wide range of dimensions to precise measurements. We carry significant volumes of stock at all times enabling us to meet large orders at short notice. We can also produce to customers exacting specifications.

Miro’s timber comes predominantly from our FSC certified sustainable plantations. The timber can be kiln dried, planed or treated upon request.

Product Description

Species Pine, Teak and Gmelina (White Teak).
Eucalyptus, Acacia and Rubber wood will also be available shortly (please make enquiry).
Width 38mm to 300mm
Thickness 19mm to 300mm
Length Up to 6.5m
