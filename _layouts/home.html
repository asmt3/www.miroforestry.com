Miro is a sustainable forestry and timber products business with plantations in Ghana and Sierra Leone, West Africa.

Miro Forestry has 10,000 hectares of standing forest established through its own planting activities

Expanding this plantation area at a rate of 3,000 hectares per year

Producing sustainable timber products including plywood, rotary veneer, poles, sawn timber and wood biomass

Our plantations are Forest Stewardship Council™ (FSC™) certified meaning we voluntarily comply with the international gold standard of responsible forest management
(see licence numbers below)

We specialise in the production of the following products:

  • Plywood, veneer and sawn timber

    Plywood (structural and film faced), rotary veneer for plywood production and sawn timber including pallets, flooring, tongue and groove

  • Poles

    Electricity utility poles, fence posts and building poles, treated and untreated.

  • Biomass Energy

    Wood biomass for power production, charcoal and firewood.
